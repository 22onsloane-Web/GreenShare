Get to know GreenShare

We’ve 10+ years of Experience in Renewable Energy

Green Share is an energy trader in South Africa.
Green Share has developed an online peer-to-peer energy trading platform to facilitate the online trading of renewable energy between buyers and sellers. The Green Share Trading Platform allows for willing buyers and sellers of renewable energy to trade power at attractive terms.

Green Electricity is offered to customers as an alternative energy source and supplements the coal-based electricity supplied by Eskom generation. Switching to Green Electricity will not change the way in which the customer is supplied with electricity but also provide additional supply stability and cost savings. Failure on the grid (operated by Eskom or the relevant municipality and over which Green Share and the green energy operator have no control) typically results in non-supply to customers.

Green Share assists with controlling and managing your Distributed Energy Resources (DERs). We assist with balancing the local grid and reduce load shedding. During Peak hours (morning and evening) when the demand for electricity is very high, the utilities and municipalities are willing to offer financial incentives to reduce consumption and generate power in order to balance the grid.
