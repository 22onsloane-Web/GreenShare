Questions

What is the energy trading platform?

Green Share’s peer-to-peer energy trading platform connects buyers and sellers of renewable energy, enabling them to trade directly online. It provides competitive pricing and a user-friendly experience for anyone looking to participate in the renewable energy market.

How does Green Electricity work?

Green Electricity integrates seamlessly with your existing supply from Eskom, acting as a supplementary energy source. It provides additional stability to your power supply, reduces costs, and doesn’t require changes to your current setup or infrastructure.

Can Green Share reduce load-shedding?

Yes, Green Share helps manage Distributed Energy Resources and balances the grid to mitigate load-shedding effects. By optimizing energy usage and generation during peak hours, it reduces strain on the grid and ensures greater reliability.

What are the cost benefits?

Green Share offers cost savings by providing affordable renewable energy options and enabling you to earn financial incentives for reducing consumption or generating power during peak demand periods.

How does it support sustainability?

By promoting renewable energy trading and reducing reliance on coal-based electricity, Green Share helps combat climate change, supports a cleaner environment, and contributes to a more sustainable future for South Africa.

Who can use Green Share’s services?

Green Share’s services are available to businesses, households, and municipalities looking to trade or use renewable energy. Whether you’re a buyer or a seller, the platform is designed to accommodate anyone seeking cost-effective and sustainable energy solutions.
